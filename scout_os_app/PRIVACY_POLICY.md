# Privacy Policy for Khasyaraka

**Last updated: January 26, 2025**

Khasyaraka ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Khasyaraka when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services and improve your learning experience in our Scout OS application.

### 1.1 Personal Information

When you register or sign in, we may collect:

- **Email Address**: Required for account creation and authentication
- **Full Name**: Your display name in the application
- **Profile Picture**: If you sign in with Google, we may collect your profile picture URL
- **Password**: Stored securely using industry-standard hashing (Argon2). We never store your password in plain text.

### 1.2 Learning and Progress Data

To provide personalized learning experiences, we collect:

- **Training Progress**: Your completion status for each training level, section, and unit
- **Quiz Scores**: Your scores, correct answers, and time spent on each quiz
- **Experience Points (XP)**: Total XP earned from completing training activities
- **Streak Data**: Daily activity streak and last active date
- **Achievement Data**: Your hack level, decrypted count, and other achievements

### 1.3 Location Information

If you use our GPS Tracker feature (part of Survival tools), we may collect:

- **Real-time Location Data**: Your current GPS coordinates, heading, speed, and altitude
- **Tracking Data**: Your movement path, distance traveled, and basecamp location
- **Location History**: Stored locally on your device for GPS tracking functionality

**Note**: Location data is used solely for the GPS Tracker feature and is processed locally on your device. We do not transmit your location data to our servers unless you explicitly use features that require location sharing.

### 1.4 Device Information

We may automatically collect certain information about your device:

- **Device Type**: Mobile device model and operating system version
- **App Version**: Version of Khasyaraka you are using
- **Technical Data**: Device identifiers, IP address (for API requests), and crash logs

This information helps us:
- Fix bugs and improve app stability
- Provide technical support
- Ensure compatibility across different devices

### 1.5 Authentication Tokens

- **JWT Tokens**: Secure authentication tokens stored locally on your device
- **Google OAuth Tokens**: If you sign in with Google, authentication tokens are managed by Google's services

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

### 2.1 Provide Core Services

- **Account Management**: Create and manage your user account
- **Authentication**: Verify your identity and maintain secure sessions
- **Learning Experience**: Track your progress, calculate XP, and unlock new content
- **Leaderboards**: Display your ranking based on XP and achievements (with your consent)

### 2.2 Improve Our Services

- **Analytics**: Analyze usage patterns to improve app features and user experience
- **Bug Fixing**: Identify and resolve technical issues
- **Performance Optimization**: Optimize app performance and reduce crashes

### 2.3 Communication

- **Account Notifications**: Send you important updates about your account or the app
- **Support**: Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage

- **Server Storage**: Your account information, progress data, and XP are stored securely on our servers
- **Local Storage**: Authentication tokens and some app preferences are stored locally on your device
- **Data Retention**: We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services

### 4.2 Security Measures

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information. We implement:

- **Encryption**: Passwords are hashed using Argon2, a secure password hashing algorithm
- **Secure Authentication**: JWT tokens with expiration for secure session management
- **HTTPS**: All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

**Important**: While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
